Annual Reports

As a non-profit organization, openness and transparency are very important to us at Ancient History Portal. 

Through our annual reports, we give our supporters and our readers the chance to see what we have achieved each year.  We also publish our financial reports to demonstrate that we've managed our finances responsibly, always in the interest of fulfilling our mission.

Unlike some foundations who channel donations into grants, our mission requires growth on a global scale. To allow for this growth, we aim for profitability year-on-year while at the same time building cash reserves to ensure the longevity of our organization. You can read more about why this is so important in this article on Linkedin.
